Helmut Lang Code Cuiron
Description

This modern scent is a minimalistic combination of leather and an energetic citrus aroma. Helmut Lang Cuiron is subtle, minimal, and very unique. There really is nothing quite like it.

Cuiron opens up with crisp bergamot and mandarin, followed subtly by leather and delicate suede. Doses of pink pepper, cassia, and cedarwood add mild complexity to the blend without going overboard.

The fragrance in Cuiron is best described as classy, reserved, and rich. The subtle leather aroma evokes images of rich, delicate suede in an old school Rolls Royce. It’s the perfect luxury cologne for the modest man.

Scent

Bergamot, mandarin, pink peppercorn, cassia, carrot seed, olibanum, labdanum, cedarwood, ambrette seed, suede accord.

Eau De Cologne
Description

Helmut Lang Eau De Cologne is a follow up to their first fragrance “Eau De Parfum” which took Europe's arthouse crowd by storm. Eau De Cologne takes the same notes found in its popular precursor, but recombines the, to make a clean, seductive, glowing follow-up fragrance.

Eau De Cologne differs from Eau De Parfum mainly in its top notes. The Parfum disguises the masculinity of its top notes, while the Cologne celebrates them. This blend has a bright, citrus top, surrounding a heart of floral gold. The base of this fragrance is dominated by musk and spicy sandalwood.

This is a beautiful signature scent suitable for nearly every occasion. Its crisp, luxurious aroma is sure to go down in history as one of the most interesting colognes ever made.

Scent

Lavender, rosemary, artemisia, orange tree notes, jasmine, rose, heliotrope, lily of the valley, cedarwood, sandalwood, vanilla, patchouli, skin accord, musk and amber

Eau De Parfum
Description

Helmut Lang specialises in beautifully minimal fashion. Since branching out to fragrances, they have maintained this elegant and minimalistic philosophy. Eau De Parfum, is their first fragrance to the market, which has taken off with a storm.

The captivating musky aroma keeps the senses interested, while the subtle top notes of citrus and heart of heliotrope, rose and jasmine fade in to take centre stage. The overall fragrance is animalistic, masculine, and ultimately timeless.

A great signature scent for the minimalist and confident man with a mysterious, seductive side.

Scent

Lavender, rosemary, artemisia, orange tree notes, jasmine, rose, heliotrope, lily of the valley, Virginian cedarwood, Indian sandalwood, vanilla, Indonesian patchouli
