R89 Safety razor
Description

Muhle R89 Safety Razors are one of the most widely used on the global market. Produced in Germany, these razors go through a strict quality assurance check before they are sold to the world.

The design uses a classic 3-piece, closed comb head to ensure the blade sits as stable as possible while you shave. Other designs use a butterfly mechanism for a slight improvement in convenience, with the tradeoff in blade stability.

Available in white, black, and chrome.

Safety Razor - Rose Gold
Description

Muhle Safety Razors (R89) are one of the popular manufacturers of premium safety razors in the world. Made in Germany, these razors must go through an intense quality assurance process before reaching the global market.

This razor uses a closed-comb design to give the blade the most stability possible. Without the moving parts of a butterfly mechanism the blade can be held tightly to the head of the safety razor.

There are a few versions of this razor, this one features a rose gold plated handle for an elegant touch to an already luxurious product.
