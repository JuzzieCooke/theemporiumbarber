Amouage Epic man
Description

Amouage Epic Man uses a blend of exotic resins, woods, and spices in this masculine and adventurous aroma. The Epic Man blend was inspired by the lives of the old silk road merchants, travelling from China into Arabia. This was a time when luxury and adventure went hand in hand. Men carried all kinds of exotic silks, teas, spices, and fragrances halfway across the globe to attain glory and riches.

The Epic man embodies this era perfectly, with top notes of pink pepper, nutmeg, cardamom, and saffron. The base is made up of leather and the highly sought after oud wood. The heart runs off a more delicate aroma of geranium and the smoky touch of frankincense and myrrh. Near the bottom lies the heavy cedarwoods and sandalwood.

This is an epic fragrance for the epic man. It’s both exotic, and sophisticated.

Scent

Pink pepper, nutmeg, cardamom, saffron, frankincense, cedarwood, patchouli, sandalwood, musk, leather, oud, geranium, myrrh.

Interlude Man
Description

Amouage Interlude Man uses a spicy, woody fragrance in a way that mimics the sensation of chaos and disorder. Interlude man speaks loudly and directly with top notes of piercing bergamot and fresh oregano. It follows up with the more subtle and informative aroma of frankincense and opopanax.

This scent is not for the faint of heart or the meek. It’s aroma has won the Basenotes Fragrance Award for “Best Niche Fragrance”, and for good reason. Its assertive fragrance has gained it a reputation among the powerful elite of Europe.

This fragrance is well balanced with sharp top notes and subtle base notes like oud, patchouli, leather, and sandalwood.

Journey man
Description

Jubilation XXV
Description

Amouage Jubilation XXV Man is a rich blend of florals, woods, and musk. It’s a masculine scent with a balanced feminine touch. The sophistication and depth of this fragrance is enough to impress even the most aromatically ignorant.

Jubilation XXV opens up with top notes of orchid, rose, and frankincense, and closes with base notes of patchouli, musk, myrrh, smoke, and moss. The heart of this fragrance is complex, with hints of clove, opopanax, bay, and honey.

This is a fragrance for the elegant and sophisticated. For the man wise enough to accentuate his feminine side rather than mask it. Doing so will only act to optimise his being, and land him a special spot in the heart of the people he surrounds himself with.

Sunshine Man
Description

Amouage Sunshine man is a bright and penetrating fragrance with top notes of aromatic florals, brandy, and bergamot. Underneath the bright and infiltrating exterior lies a heart of refreshing juniper, lavender, and clary sage. Tying it all together is a grounding base of smoky cedar, tonka bean, and tobacco.

This fragrance is well balanced overall, with bright penetrating notes emanating out from the beginning, just as the sun does each morning. The aroma slowly fades to a heavier, more masculine and subtle aroma of wood smoke, tobacco, and sweet tonka bean as the day progresses.

This fragrance tells a story throughout the day, as it peaks and fades. The mornings productive, and the evenings dark and pleasurable, just like the man who wears it.

Scent

Brandy, orange, lavender, bergamot, lime, juniper, oregano, vanilla, tonka bean, cedar, fresh tobacco, and clary sage.
