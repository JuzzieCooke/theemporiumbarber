Neotantric Fragrances Code Dirty Money
Description

This distinct masculine fragrance by Neotantric is both passionate and mysterious. Dirty Money uses spicy nutmeg and peppercorn overtones, clouded with woody cedar, vetiver, and tobacco.

The paradox of this aroma is that it’s both subtle and intense. It’s the type of fragrance that deserves a second whiff, and one that tends to remain in the memory. The mysteriousness of it keeps people coming back for more.

Scent

Lemon, pink pepper, saffron, jasmine, nutmeg, black pepper, cedarwood, vetiver, amber, and tobacco.

Perfumer

Serge Majoullier

Inferno E Paradiso
Description

Inferno E Paradiso is the first unisex fragrance by Neotantric Fragrances. This well-balanced fragrance combines spicy black pepper and bergamot top notes, a saffron and plum heart, and a base of patchouli, amber, and tonka bean.

This scent makes a perfect gift for the aromatically inclined. It appeals to both men and women and is a perfect balance between the two sexes. The grounding, masculine base notes tend to enter later in the day, while the morning emits more uplifting and feminine aromas.

Scent

Bergamot, black pepper, saffron, cedarwood, incense, plum, patchouli, sandalwood, amber and tonka bean.

Perfumer

Mathilde Bijaoui

Killer Honey
Description

Killer Honey by Neotantric Fragrances has a uniquely fluid nature. It morphs from playful floral and spice early on to musky patchouli and the deep aroma of ylang-ylang. This complex aroma is highly floral but is balanced out well with the masculine base notes of patchouli and musk.

The ylang-ylang is at least partly responsible for the transition in aroma, as it changes throughout the day its persuasive nature convinces the rest of the scent to follow suit.

This is a great scent for those with two sides. Fresh and vibrant in the morning, and subtle and calm in the afternoon.

Scent

Ylang-ylang, pink peppercorns, elemi, jasmine, patchouli and peach, rose, rockrose, musk and benzoin.

Perfumer

Celine Ripert

100 ml Bottle

Made In

Sweden

Manic Love Man
Description

Manic Love Man by Neotantric Fragrances is an overpowering, spicy aroma suitable for both men and women. Its irrefutable fragrance speaks loudly with an orgy of hysterical aromatics.

Piercing bergamot, cedar leaf, and lavender amalgamate with cypress and freesia. Deep, penetrating notes of vanilla, musk, moss and patchouli then mingle with the array of sensual top notes to create by far one of the most unique and intense scents we have seen in a long time.

Pampara Peep Show
Description

Parampara Peep Show is a devious blend of rosemary and musk. The aroma is intoxicating and penetrating tends to have a surprising effect on one's behaviour.

Parampara denotes the succession from guru to disciple. It’s the uninterrupted continuation of a teaching. Parampara Peep Show passes along unscrupulous pleasure with its subtle aroma.

Top notes of lemon and bergamot meet the soul of rosemary and musk in this refreshingly masculine aroma.

Lemon, bergamot, mandarin, tangerine, orange, apple, pineapple, grapefruit, eucalyptus, lavandin, caraway, cistus, rosemary, litsea cubeba, cedarwood, rich dry amber, rich musk, leather, guaiac wood and vanilla.
