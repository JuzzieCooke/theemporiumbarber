American Crew Defining Paste
Description

The defining paste by American Crew contains natural, waxy constituents like beeswax and lanolin to provide texture and a medium level of hold for the hair. It has a matte finish suitable for both long, and short hair.

The goal of this product is to add definition and texture while allowing some level of control over the style of the hair.

Styling Notes

Medium hold, matte finish.

How to use

Add a small amount to the palm of the hands and warm up by rubbing the palm of the hands together to melt the wax. This allows the product to evenly distribute through the hair more easily.

Aqua, Lanolin Cera (Lanolin Wax), Cetearyl Alcohol, Glycerin, Cera Alba (Beeswax), Tribehenin, Tridecyl Stearate, Ceteareth-25, Tridecyl Trimellitate, Alcohol Denat., VP/VA Copolymer, Dipentaerythrityl Hexacaprylate/Hexacaprate, Acrylates/C10-30 Alkyl Acrylate Crosspolymer, Melaleuca Alternifolia (Tea Tree) Leaf Oil, Triethanolamine, Tetrasodium EDTA, Sambucus Nigra Flower Extract, Camellia Sinensis Leaf Extract, Urtica Dioica (Nettle) Extract, Symphytum Officinale Leaf Extract, Chondrus Chrispus (Carrageenan) Extract, Echinacea Angustifolia Extract, Salvia Hispanica Seed Extract, Benzophenone-3, Parfum, Benzyl Benzoate, Benzyl Cinnamate, Limonene, Linalool, Hydroxyisohexyl 3-Cyclohexene Carboxaldehyde, Butylphenyl Methylpropional, Alpha-Isomethyl Ionone, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylparaben, Methylparaben, Bentonite, Mica, CI 77891 (Titanium Dioxide), CI 77491 (Iron Oxides).
