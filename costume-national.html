Costume National Code 21
Description

Costume National 21 was designed to commemorate the company's 21st anniversary. It’s a warm, sensual aroma to increase desire and temptation. Top notes of bergamot penetrate the senses, while a heart of clary sage, saffron, and royal jelly speak to the heart. The long-lasting base of 21 uses scents from patchouli, sandalwood, cedarwood, cashmere woods, tonka bean, and the rare and exotic oud wood.

Scent

Bergamot, milk, orange blossom, moss, clary sage, pepper, saffron, royal jelly, cashmere woods, cedarwood, oud wood, patchouli, sandalwood, vetiver, amber, rockrose, musk, tonka bean, vanilla.

Perfumer

Anne Flipo, Béatrice Piquet, Juliette Karagueuzoglou.

Homme
Description

Costume National Homme uses a harmonic blend of citrus, and spiced wood to produce a sensual yet masculine fragrance. This is Costume National’s very first dedicated men's fragrance. Since its creation in 2009. It has risen to the top of the ranks in men's fragrances, winning European Men's Fragrance of the year, as well as the Instyle Awards for Best Woody Oriental in 2010.

This scent is timeless, seductive, and undeniably masculine. It’s a perfect scent for the modern man.

Scent

Spiced wood, cinnamon, cardamom, bergamot, balsamic, thyme, cloves, lime, patchouli, sandalwood, labdanum, and citrus.

Perfumer

Dominique Ropion
