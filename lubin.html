Lubin Code Idole De Lubin
Description

Idole De Lubin Eau De Parfum is a twist on the already famous Eau De Toilette fragrance by Lubin. This adventurous aroma was made to embody the fearless souls of our past. For adventurers who would boldly go where nobody else would dream of going.

Its character is bold and adventurous, with elements of amber, frankincense, and rockrose at the tip, and ebony, rum and leather at the hilt. This scent is suitable for both men and women. Its balance between feminine desire and curiosity blends well with bold and dangerous masculine traits.

Scent

Rum, saffron, black cumin, bitter orange peel, rock rose, smoked ebony, frankincense, sugar cane, leather, sandalwood, and amber

Upper Ten
Description

Lubin Upper Ten was inspired by the sharp rise in American industrial authority by the hands of the captains of industry in the 1800â€™s. The poet Nathaniel Parker Willis coined the term in 1850. The Upper Ten (or upper Ten Thousand), refers to the elite, self made aristocrats that made the industrial revolution possible. The immense success of these individuals lead to a unique and powerful culture, which exists to this day.

Lubin Upper Ten was originally launched in 1920, nut has been reimagined and updated to fit a 21st century nose. The aroma contains notes of cedar, sandalwood, leather, geranium, cardamom, spices, and cinnamon.

No other scent celebrates success like Lubin Upper Ten.

Korrigan EDP
Description

Lubin Korrigan EDP is an intimate array of caramel and wood. The sweet, yet masculine aroma is both tempting and satisfying. Caramel itself evokes the desire for unending pleasure and a loss of control of the senses. It speaks to men and women, in the same way, whisper, draw closer, and pursue pleasures of the most extravagant kind.

The woodiness of this fragrance keeps it mellow and brings a certain masculinity no other aroma can deliver. The whisky and earthy nature keeps us grounded and locked in.

Korrigan EDP opens up with the sweet, penetrating aroma of caramel and cognac, which is quickly followed up by a heart of lavender, ambrette, and whisky. The woody base contains notes of the rare and exotic oud wood, cedar, vetiver, leather, and musk.

This scent is perfect for the man who works hard by day, in order to play hard at night.

Scent

Juniper berry, saffron, cognac, Lavender, ambrette, whiskey, Cedar, oud, leather, vetiver, musk.

Akkad
Description

Lubin Akkad is a sensual, luminous, amber based fragrance inspired by the profane pleasures of ancient tyrants. Its spicy citrus entrance is sure to leave an impression. Balanced out with milder notes of clary sage and frankincense in an elegant display of botanical synergy Akkad is a sophisticated and premium fragrance.

The base of this fragrance stands on patchouli, vanilla, elemi, and 2 different kinds of amber.
