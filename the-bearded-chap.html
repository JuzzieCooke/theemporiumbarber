The Bearded Chap Code Bluebeards Revenge Shaving Cream
Description

Bluebeards Revenge Shave Cream creates a fine lather with or without a shaving brush. It also contains a formula known as decelerine which slows the growth of hair on the face. This helps to extend the duration of your shave.

Recommended for use with Bluebeards Revenge pre-shave cream, and post-shave cream.

Classic English barbershop scent.

How To Use

Using either your hands or a shaving brush, work up a fine lather and apply to the face. Leave on for 2-3 minutes to soften the hair before shaving. Rinse and apply Bluebeards Revenge post-shave cream for best results.
