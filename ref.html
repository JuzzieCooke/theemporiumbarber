REF. Code Dry Shampoo 204
Description

REF. 204 Dry Shampoo 204 is a dry, weightless powder spray designed to add volume and clean the hair. It has the added ability of being able to style the hair, add body, and allow for maximum style re-workability.

This dual-purpose dry shampoo saves time and effort without any reduction in quality. It makes for a perfect addition to the gym bag, beach bag, or keeping around the office.

This product also comes in brown, and black dry shampoos as well to give the best colour support possible for your specific hair.

Hair Types

Suitable for all hair types.

How To Use

Spray the dry shampoo 204 powder directly into the hair and massage into the scalp.

Size

200 ml Spray Bottle
